Detailed Description

The GenericAudioSampleSequence specification and body defines a single sequence type for audio samples with zero-based arbitrary indexed access to positions in the sequence called AudioSampleList and a list of those audio sample lists called AudioSampleBuffer.

It is extensible in one dimension (list of samples) while the other (list of channels) is fixed.
