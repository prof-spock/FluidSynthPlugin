Detailed Description

template<typename AudioSample>
struct Audio::GenericAudioSampleBuffer< AudioSample >

A GenericAudioSampleBuffer is a multidimensional sequence type for audio samples with zero-based arbitrary indexed access to positions in the sequence.

It is extensible in one dimension (list of samples) while the other (list of channels) is fixed upon construction.
